Projects

Gillytech — Collaborative STEM Reasoning Platform

A reasoning-first, real-time collaborative learning platform for Kenyan CBC classrooms, in which students must record their reasoning before answer options unlock, discuss in small groups, and submit against server-side scoring. Architected as a layered modular monolith (Express routes → auth middleware → repository layer → data store) with an in-process, session- and group-scoped WebSocket overlay for chat, presence and question-advance events. Ten architecturally significant requirements were derived from the SRS and mapped to the style, with trade-offs — single-process scaling, non-transactional first-correct awards, read-time analytics — each recorded against an explicit revisit trigger across six ADRs. Documentation published at davidkabera.github.io/gilli-tech.

My Projects

Gillytech

A reasoning-first, real-time collaborative STEM platform for Kenyan CBC classrooms. Students write their reasoning before answer options unlock, discuss in groups over WebSockets, and submit against server-side scoring. Built as a layered modular monolith with an event-driven real-time overlay, documented through ten ASRs and six ADRs.

Talos Kubernetes Cluster & Federated Learning

A multi-node Kubernetes cluster on Talos Linux, running a federated learning pipeline for MNIST image classification with parallel training workers, Redis-based gradient coordination, shared persistent storage, and a federated-averaging aggregation job.

MundaMail — Government Messaging System

A government email platform designed from requirements upward: an IEEE 830 SRS, architecturally significant requirements, architecture decision records, and a C1–C4 LikeC4 model of a modular monolith with a Kafka event broker and tiered audit persistence.

Stream Cipher Performance Analysis

MSc thesis research benchmarking five stream ciphers across a century of design — Vernam, RC4, ChaCha20, Grain-128AEAD and the Dömösi–Horváth automaton cipher — through an original Constraint-Principle Framework.
